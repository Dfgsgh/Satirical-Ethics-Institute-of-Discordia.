Mission Statement

The Department of Boost Prevention (DBP) exists to detect, investigate, and shun the selfish act of boosting one’s own Discord server. We believe server growth should be organic, awkward, and riddled with poorly timed memes — not Nitro-funded trickery.

What Counts As a Self-Boost?

According to our in-house moral algorithm (a dice and a feeling), the following are examples of unacceptable behavior:

  • Boosting your own server with your main account
  • Creating a second account just to boost
  • “Just testing the badge”
  • Starting your own cult a not getting all members to boost at once
  • Bribing relatives with pizza to boost your Roblox clan server
Reminder: Every self-boost brings us closer to the heat death of online authenticity.
