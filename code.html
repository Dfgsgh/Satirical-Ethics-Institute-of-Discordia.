Preamble

The Satirical Ethics Institute of Discordia is committed to fostering an environment of approximate decency, interpretive morality, and sporadic accountability. This Code outlines what is expected, generally discouraged, or legally fascinating.

“Clarity is the enemy of plausible deniability.” — Internal Motto (Unofficial)

Core Principles (loosely defined)

  • 1. Be Nice (unless you're being ironic).
  • 2. Don’t Spam. Unless it’s funny. Then maybe.
  • 3. Respect authority. Unless you're pretty sure they're wrong.
  • 4. Never boost your own server... unless it’s on fire and the boost is a metaphorical extinguisher.
  • 5. Keep channel names meaningful but not too meaningful.
  • 6. Never engage in digital necromancy (reviving dead threads). Unless it’s for drama.

Enforcement Procedures

Violations of this Code will be reviewed by the Council of Internal Review (a rotating group of whichever staff are awake). Punishments may include:

  • A strongly worded emoji reaction
  • Temporary exile to the #timeout-void
  • Forced reading of the Terms of Service out loud in voice chat
  • In extreme cases, public shaming using vintage Discord stickers

Appeals Process

If you feel you’ve been unfairly judged, please file a complaint with our Department of Righteous Regret. Response time may vary from 3 hours to 7 eternities.
