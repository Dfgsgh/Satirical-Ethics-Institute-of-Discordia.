Welcome to the Institute

Founded in the year 2025 (and also kind of as a joke), the Satirical Ethics Institute of Discordia has been tirelessly dedicated to uncovering the most ethically questionable behaviors across digital platforms — starting with the most heinous of all: boosting your own Discord server.

“Just because you can give yourself Nitro perks doesn’t mean you should.” — Chairman of Ethics, probably

We are a pioneering organization with a bold mission: to say things that sound important, about things that absolutely aren’t. Whether you're a rogue moderator, a self-boosting baron, or someone who just joined to steal emotes, you're part of the system — and we’re watching that system with binoculars from a cardboard fort labeled research lab.

Our Goals

  • To classify all forms of Discord server misconduct, with special focus on unnecessary pings and poor emoji-to-channel ratios.
  • To publish peer-reviewed articles like "The Philosophical Implications of Server Vanity URLs".
  • To provide absolutely no real legal advice, ever.
